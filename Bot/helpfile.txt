Available Commands:

addm => add members
  Ex: addm member1,member2

showm => show members

addt => add transactions
  Ex: addt paid 10 for dinner. member1,member2
      addt member1 paid 10 for Lunch@McD. member1,member2
      addt I owe member1 10
      addt member1,member2 owe membern 10

showt => displays current month transactions unless month is specified
  Ex: showt
      showt jan

help => displays this message

settle => Show computed settlements
